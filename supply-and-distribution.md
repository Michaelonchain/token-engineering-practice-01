# Supply & Distribution

## Total Supply
1,000,000,000 tokens

This supply was chosen for simplicity, scalability, and clarity.

---

## Initial Distribution

- Community & Rewards: 40%
- Team: 20%
- Investors: 15%
- Treasury: 15%
- Liquidity: 10%

This structure prioritizes community participation while maintaining
long-term sustainability through vesting and treasury reserves.

---

## Vesting Rationale

Team and investor allocations are vested to:
- Prevent early dumping
- Reduce inflation shock
- Align long-term incentives
